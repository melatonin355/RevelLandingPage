Motivation

In the restaurant industry, waiters have a clear incentive to up-sell often and put customer satisfaction above all else. Waiters that do these things get more tips, and the restaurant profits from increased volume. Unfortunately, convenient store and gas station clerks have no such incentives. Our app empowers these workers to feel engaged and have concrete reasons to up-sell and increase volume of high-margin items.

We accomplish this by making use of the Revelry API sales summary and backend report endpoints. We pull weekly and daily analysis of specific products and their rankings based on quantity sold as well as profit margin. By combining these two metrics we identify high profit margin items that are also lagging behind in quantity sold. Once these products have been identified, we introduce them to clerks and employees in an easy to understand graphic and create a leaderboard that incentives them to take action to increase sales of the target item, or “Item in Focus” for the week. Employees are encouraged to up-sell said item at the register, rearrange store layout, or create temporary discounts depending on the situation and product. Top producers are rewarded weekly with cash prizes, gift-cards, and other perks.

We offer a solution to incentivize traditionally unskilled workers in an extremely difficult job, aligning them with corporate goals for the first time. The combination of Shell’s reward programs, Revelry’s innovative POS solutions and robust APIs, combined with Paideia Group’s employee rewards program will not only increase revenue and margins, but in the long run will attract higher quality workers than Shell’s competitors. Employees want to work in an environment where their merit results in personal gain. We look forward to innovating further to unlock more insights and create more gamification options for the workers who use Revelry and our app.
